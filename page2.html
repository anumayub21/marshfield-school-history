Chapel Green Board School

In 1873 the residents of Chapel Green petitioned the Bradford School Board for a new school. A survey of the area was carried out, and plans were drawn up for both the ground floor and the upper floor.

The school logbooks provide detailed accounts of daily life. The Infants logbook (1877–1905 Infants) and the Junior logbook (1877–1910 Junior) are available to view. The earlier Church School logbook (Logbook 1868–1903) also contains valuable information.

Notable Dates

1870 – Bradford set up the Bradford School Board.

1873 – Chapel Green residents petitioned for a school.

1875 – Construction planning and surveys completed.
